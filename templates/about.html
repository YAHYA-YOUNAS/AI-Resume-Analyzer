What is Resume Analyzer?

Resume Analyzer is an AI-powered tool designed for both job seekers and recruiters. For job seekers, it helps optimize resumes for specific job categories by analyzing content and providing a score based on alignment with target job category. For recruiters, it streamlines the candidate shortlisting process by evaluating and scoring resumes to identify the most suitable candidates quickly and efficiently.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Analysis:  Uses advanced deep learning to evaluate resume content
  • Job-Specific Scoring:  Scores your resume against specific job categories
  • Multiple Format Support:  Accepts PDF, DOCX, DOC, and plain text resumes
  • Privacy Focused:  Your documents are processed securely and never stored

Understanding Your Score

Your resume score is calculated based on how well your skills, experience, and qualifications match the keywords for your selected job category.

9-10 Excellent match Excellent! Your resume is highly optimized for this position
7-8 Good match Great job! Your resume matches well with this job category
5-6 Fair match Good start. Consider enhancing key skills and experiences
3-4 Weak match Your resume needs improvement to be competitive for this role
0-2 Poor match Your resume requires significant updates to match this job category

Tip: For best results, tailor your resume to each specific job application by emphasizing relevant skills and experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my resume data stored?

No, we process your resume in real-time and do not store any of your personal information or resume content after analysis is complete.

What file formats are supported?

We support PDF, DOC, and DOCX files, as well as plain text pasted directly into the text box.

How accurate is the scoring?

Our scoring is based on analysis of thousands of successful resumes, but should be used as a guideline rather than an absolute measure.

Can I analyze multiple versions of my resume?

Yes! We encourage you to test different versions to see which scores highest for your target job.
